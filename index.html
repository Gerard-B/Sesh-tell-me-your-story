Sesh: Tell Me Your Story

My podcast was created in 2020. After living abroad for 8 years in the last 20 years, I wanted to create a place to hear some real life stories from all over the world. These stories are real, personal and from people I know and love.
Hosted by Seshnie Taylor.

Engaging & Positive.

The podcast is focused on interesting stories in other peoples life, and although we sometimes touch upon dark experiences, we always try to focus on the good and the lessons we can learn from it. Every podcast ends with a poem from me or a guest.

  • Type of guests: People I met during my travels
  • Structure:Opening, deep conversation, poem
  • Release date: New episode every sometimes (I try 1 episode per month)
  • Category: Entertainment, Spirituality, Education, Art

Feel free to click any of the podcasts below and start listening.

About me

My name is Seshnie and I am an avid learner, connecter and sharer. Below you can find some information about me, and if you want to go straight to my episodes: you can subscribe via the icons above, or click on the episode-links on this page.

Academic & Sharer.

All my life I like to learn. And in those years I met some amazing people and I would like to share their stories with you.

  • Birthday: 1991
  • Nationality: Born in South-Africa, grew up in the United Kingdom
  • Country: Australia
  • Degree: French and Chinese Languages Bachelor and International Relations Master Degree
  • Possibly a section with goals for 2020 and how you are doing, and a section for 2021? Touch typing: 75wpm

I moved at 10 years old from South-Africa to the United Kingdom. Here I found my passion in education. I went to Nottingham university which offered me the change to further my language skills in China. In China I established my tutoring company. At the start of 2020 I decided I wanted to find a place to settle for the coming decades and it was a life-long dream to move to Australia.
